📋 蒙多五步法
1

定位瓶颈

不知道怎么做？做不好？不够好？先精准定位问题本质。蒙多不盲目行动。

2

多渠道并行搜索

同时向 ChatGPT、Claude、Gemini、DeepSeek 请教。同时搜索 GitHub、Stack Overflow、掘金、官方文档。不依赖单一来源。

3

四维对比

对收集到的 2-3 种方案，按简洁度、性能、可维护性、匹配度四个维度打分对比。选出最优方案。

4

吸收实施

理解 → 适配 → 代码落实。绝不直接复制粘贴未理解的代码。适配到当前项目的技术栈和规范。

5

效果验证 + 沉淀

解决了吗？有副作用吗？有价值的方案用 skill_manage 沉淀为可复用的 Skill。下次瞬间解决。

📋 Mundo's Five-Step Method
1

Identify the Bottleneck

Don't know how? Can't do it well? Not good enough? Pinpoint the exact problem. Mundo never acts blindly.

2

Multi-Channel Parallel Search

Simultaneously consult ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek. Search GitHub, Stack Overflow, Dev.to, official docs. Never rely on a single source.

3

Four-Dimension Comparison

Compare 2-3 solutions across simplicity, performance, maintainability, and fit. Select the optimal one.

4

Absorb & Implement

Understand → Adapt → Code. Never copy-paste uncomprehended code. Adapt to current project's tech stack and conventions.

5

Verify & Preserve

Did it work? Any side effects? Valuable solutions saved as reusable Skills via skill_manage. Instant solve next time.
